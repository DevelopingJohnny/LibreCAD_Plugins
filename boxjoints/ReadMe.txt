BoxJoints

It's a plugin for LibreCAD.
Programmed for creating 2D Joints to make boxes & lasercut them.

Demonstaion Video on Youtube:
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4isa_GsPL4Y

Usage:
1. Select "BoxJoints" in "Plugins"
2. Configure your Preferences in Pop-up Window.
3. Select 2 Lines to join them together.
4. Select Orientation of thoes 2 Joints/ Lines-Side Outside of box.
  ** Plugin generates 2 Joints based of Selection **
